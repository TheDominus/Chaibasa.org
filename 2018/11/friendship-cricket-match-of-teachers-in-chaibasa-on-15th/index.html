Friendship Cricket Match of teachers in Chaibasa on 15th

On 15th November, a Friendship Cricket match has been organized between teachers of primary vs. middle and middle school teachers of West Bank in Birsa Munda Cricket Stadium ground on the occasion of Lord Birsa Munda’s birth anniversary and Jharkhand State Foundation Day.

To participate in the said match, the primary school teachers’ team will be selected in the Birsa Munda Cricket Stadium ground on Wednesday. It is mandatory for the participating teachers to bring their teacher identity card with them. The selection committee formed by the District Cricket Association will be selected by both teams. The players of both the selected teams will report on the Birsa Munda Cricket Stadium ground at 10 am on November 15. The District Education Officer and District Superintendent of Police will jointly inaugurate the match organized by the Department of District Education and Literacy while the Deputy Commissioner will be rewarded as a Chief Guest in the closing ceremony.
