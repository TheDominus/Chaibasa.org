Annual Sports Day was organized on behalf of Saint Xavier’s English Medium School and Junior College on Wednesday at the Association Ground of Chaibasa. As the chief guest in the program, Deputy Commissioner Arwa Rajkamal, Dyasis head office in the special guest, Reverged Father of the Jamshedpur, Augustine Topon, the dignitary guest, SDPO of the district Amar Kumar Pandey was present. The program was inaugurated by the Deputy Commissioner. In order to make the program successful, the Deputy Commissioner remembered the days of his school while praising the Principal of the Vidyalaya, Sister Sheetal, teachers and parents. In addition, all children are encouraged to participate in any sport with interest. This will enable the children to succeed in all spheres of life. On the spot, the children got a lot of enthusiasm in the children’s ball ball, relay race, sack race, cock battle race, apple race. Children’s Dance Program was also organized for entertainment along with sports. In this class, children of class 2, 3, were presented saris, charming dance by children of dance dance and children of 7,8,9 by Pam-Pam Dance, Class 4,5,6 children. In the event, the Acura House Yellow Team was the Overall Champion.<\/p>\n
