Total 9 candidates are participating for Lok sabha elections from Chaibasa constituency this year. Here is the name of candidates along with the election symbols and the name of the party<\/p>\n\n\n\n

  • Gita Koda – Hand – Indian National Congress (INC)<\/li>
  • Paradeshi Lal Munda – Elephant – Bahujan Samaj Party (BSP)<\/li>
  • Laxman Giluva – Lotus – Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) <\/li>
  • Krishna Mardi – Bottle – Jharkhand Mukti Morcha (JMM)<\/li>
  • Chandramohan Hembrum – Torch – Socialist Unity Centre of India (SUCI)<\/li>
  • Pratap Singh Banara – Coat – Ambedkarite Party<\/li>
  • Hari Urao – Bat – Kalinga Sena<\/li>
  • Anil Soren – Farmer driving Tractor – Independent<\/li>
  • Pushpa Sinku – Helicopter – Independent<\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n

    Do vote this election, if you don’t like any one from the above, Press NOTA (None of the Above) <\/p>\n\n\n\n

    Do you know? <\/p>\n\n\n\n

    You don’t choose the Prime Minister for the country , the Lok Sabha candidates choose the Prime Minister for the country. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

    So, keep in mind while choosing the candidate who will benefit you directly, not only the seeing the prime minister.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
