Chaibasa IAS officer has put his effort and his enginnering mind set to create low cost innovation to prevent the spread of coronavirus in West Singhbhum.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Phone Booth<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

In the Sadar Hospital, Chaibasa, a sample collection center was inaugurated on 4th April’20. It will be used to collect nasal and throat swab samples for Covid-19 Testing. This not only reduces the manpower but also provides protection of the health personnel from any expected COVID-19 patient. It also cut down the need for the PPE.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Co-Bot<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

The remote-controlled robots will serve food and medicines to Covid-19 Patients in two hospitals in West Singbhum district from 14th April’20. This step was taken to protect the doctor and health worker from getting affected with the coronavirus disease, COVID-19<\/p>\n\n\n\n

<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Touch-Free Wash Basin<\/strong><\/h2>\n\n\n\n

A washbasin is installed in the district dedicated COVID-19 hospital to prevent touching the washbasin. This will provide safety to health personnel and visitors to the hospital.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
