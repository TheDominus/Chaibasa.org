The Annual Festival of Education School of English was celebrated on Sunday with cultural programs. As the Chief Guest of the function, Dr. Ranjit Prasad, Director of Human Resources Development Institute, Chaibasa, was invited. Together with the special guest Federation of Jharkhand Chamber of Commerce Industries, Kolhan Vice President Vikas Mishra and President of West Kashmir Chamber of Commerce Industries Bajrang Lal Chirania and Chairman of Red Cross Society Kishor Prasad launched the lamp lighting program. The festival was started by the students of the school by presenting Saraswati Vandana and Saraswati Vandana dance. After that, pop dance was presented by the brothers of LKG, including Krishna Mahato, IS Kumar Nayak, Navneet Pradhan, Rohan Das, Gora Hasada, Shraddha Kesari, Kshaskar Kadali, Smriti, Jannavi Nayak etc.. After that, “Mere Papa” song was danced by the children. After that, Bihar’s dance music, Sambalpuri Dance of Odisha, Nagpuri Dance of Chota Nagpur and local Ho Dance etc. were staged.<\/p>\n
