Applications for 1600 posts have been invited by Sabari Group, Bangalore in the said recruitment camp cum employment fair. Eligible beneficiaries of the District whose qualifications are Diploma, ITI 10th, 12th pass, Diploma in Electronics and Communication, they can participate in this recruitment camp cum employment fair. Selected beneficiaries will be provided employment at monthly salary of 13,000 to 15000 rupees in Chennai and Bengaluru.<\/p>\n
